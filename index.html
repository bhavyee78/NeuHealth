Why Families Trust Us

More Than a Clinic.
A Complete Support System.

Most clinics give you a report and say goodbye. We give you a report and show you the way forward.

CQC-Standard Reports

We are completing our CQC registration to ensure our assessments meet the highest national standards — recognised by the NHS, schools and employers.

Built-In Legal Expertise

Our unique legal background means we don't just diagnose — we fight for your EHCP, secure funding, and challenge the Council when they say no. All in one place.

We See the Whole Person

We assess your strengths as well as your challenges. Our reports are written in plain English with clear next steps for school, work and life — not just medical jargon.

No Long NHS Waits

NHS waiting lists can stretch to years. We aim to see you within weeks, with all assessments and reports completed within 8 weeks of receiving your information.

No Hidden Fees

The price you see includes your clinical sessions, standardised tests, and the final written report. We also offer 0% interest payment plans and require only a 20% deposit.

Sensory-Friendly Approach

Our in-person clinics are designed to be calm, quiet and sensory-friendly. We offer online appointments for those who feel safer at home, and always go at your pace.

The Process

Simple as 1-2-3

No confusing paperwork. No medical jargon. Just clear steps to get you the answers you deserve.

1

The Chat

A free call to make sure you are booking the right assessment for your needs. We listen before we advise.

2

The Assessment Day

A relaxed meeting — in person at our sensory-friendly clinic or online from the safety of your home — with our expert clinical team.

3

The Plan

A one-to-one session to explain your report in plain English and map out your legal, educational and financial next steps.
