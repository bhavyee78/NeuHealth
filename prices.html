💡 Top Tips for Parents

Don't “Prep” the School

Just let the teachers know we are coming to help. We want to see a normal school day to get an accurate picture.

Be Yourself at Home

Don't worry about the washing up or the house being messy! We are there to support your child, not judge your home.

We Handle the Talk

If you find it hard to talk to teachers, don't worry. We will do the “heavy lifting” and explain the science and the law to them for you.
